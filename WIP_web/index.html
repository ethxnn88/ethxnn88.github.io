Who am I?

Hello! My name is Ethan, a tech enthusiast who goes by the alias of ethxnn88 online. I'm intrigued about how electronics work, and interested in the engineering process of them.

I'm also interested in software development, and have a bit of experience writing in languages such as python and objective-c++. I'd love to learn more about writing swift and using Xcode more, and delving into SwiftUI and UI design in general.

Some personal hobbies of mine are collecting older "rare" and "legacy" Apple devices, such as iPods, iPads, and most importantly iPhones. With my coding knowledge, I write iOS Jailbreak tweaks whenever I have an idea of something I'd like different on one of my old Jailbroken iDevices. So far, I've only perfected and released one of my many tweaks, but I have plans to release more in the future. I also purchase damaged and sometimes even nonfunctional Apple devices from eBay, and proceeding to repair and re-sell them for a profit.

In addition, I run a YouTube channel where I make videos showcasing these devices, such as unboxing, jailbreaking, downgrading or repairing them. I often make videos unboxing and reviewing new and somewhat-old devices, and also make videos about the unboxing and repair process of my beforementioned eBay flips.

I also sometimes spend my free time delving into complicated projects such as my iPhone 5C iOS 6 port project, where after an unnecessary amount of struggling led to me sucessfully booting iOS 6 on an iPhone 5C.

This website was also one of my projects. I was interested in getting into web design, and I came to the conclusion that the easiest way to get started was to write my own website.

Feel free to check out some of my other projects and guides using the buttons in the menu bar, and check out my socials at the bottom of the page.

Have a nice day!
